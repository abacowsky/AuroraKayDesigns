About Me

Hi there! I’m Aurora—the designer behind Ky Designs. I specialize in creative branding and web design for bold, small businesses that are ready to stand out. I’m based in the beautiful Inland Northwest, where I spend as much time as I can outdoors. Whether I’m hiking, camping, or just chasing sunshine with my son and our animals, nature is a big part of what keeps me inspired. I’m a recent graphic and web design grad, a proud boy mom, and a total animal lover. When I’m not designing, you’ll probably find me exploring the mountains, sipping iced coffee, or sketching new ideas with my dogs curled up nearby. Let’s make something awesome together!

My Work

Silverwood X Pepsi Semi Truck Wrap

Winner of a student design contest, this semi truck wrap was created for a Pepsi and Silverwood Theme Park collaboration. I designed both sides of the trailer to capture the bold, high-energy spirit of the brands. The final design was selected and produced for real-world use.

Free Rein | 2024 AAF Create

My team won 1st place in AAF Spokane’s Create competition for our work with Free Rein, a nonprofit focused on horses empowering humans. We designed a bold visual package—including a banner, social posts, and custom illustrations—to capture the strength and heart of their mission.

PIA Print Contest | Print is Persuasive

This poster explores how print media influences public opinion and shapes social movements. It includes imagery related to recent events like the overturning of Roe v. Wade, the Capitol riot, and themes from 1984 to highlight print’s role in persuasion and collective action. The design was selected as the winning College entry among 70 students.
